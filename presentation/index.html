05

Automation & Operating Rules

Community Edition workaround + Non-negotiable rules

Activity-Based Automation

R1
New lead created

→ "Qualify Lead within 24 hours"

R2
Stage = New Requirement (P1)

→ "Submit 5 candidates in 48 hours"

R3
Stage = Discovery (P2)

→ "Prep checklist + schedule call"

R4
No activity > 5 days

→ "Re-engage prospect" alert to Manager

4 Non-Negotiable Rules

01
Everything Enters CRM First

No Excel, no Notion, no sticky notes. Zero exceptions.

02
No Deal Exists Outside Odoo

Email/spreadsheet tracking = system failure → escalate.

03
Every Opportunity: 4 Fields

Owner + Value + Stage + Next Action — always.

04
No Inactive Deals > 7 Days

Auto-alert fired, reviewed in next standup.

🎤 Speaker Notes

"Since we're on Community Edition, we don't have enterprise automation. We use Odoo's Activity system instead — four rules that keep the team accountable. And four non-negotiable operating rules from your platform document. These are enforced from day one. One thing I want to confirm: the automation triggers at 5 days, but the operating rule says 7 days — should we align these?"
